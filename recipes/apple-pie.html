Apple Pie

A receipe for Apple pie like Grandma used to make!

Ingredients:

  • Pie Crust
  • Apples
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Flour
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Lemon
  • Egg
  • Onion
  • Tomato Paste
  • Partmesan

Directions:

  1. Prepare Crust
  2. Chop Apples and Mix with Sugar/Cinnamon
  3. Fill Inside of Pie Crust
  4. Cover top with dough
  5. Bake
